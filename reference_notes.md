# BlokesTrips Reference Site Analysis

## Colour Palette
- Background (dark): near-black / very dark charcoal (#0d0d0d or similar)
- Accent: Bold amber/orange (#F5A623 or similar - used for italic headings, CTA buttons, price text)
- Text: White on dark sections
- Card backgrounds: Dark charcoal with subtle borders
- Section accent: Orange/amber full-width banner ("Think Group Chat Chaos" section)
- CTA buttons: Amber/orange fill with black text, or outlined white

## Typography
- Headings: Bold italic condensed sans-serif (Impact-like or Barlow Condensed Bold Italic)
- Logo: Bold condensed, "BLOKES" in white, "TRIPS" in amber/orange
- Body: Clean sans-serif, medium weight
- Section labels: Small uppercase tracking (e.g., "PROCESS", "OUR EXPERIENCES")
- Numbers (01, 02...): Large ghost/outline style in background

## Layout & Structure
### Navigation
- Dark/transparent sticky nav
- Logo left, nav links centre, CTA button right (amber)
- Nav links: How It Works, Packages, Franchise, Reviews

### Hero Section
- Full-screen dark background with silhouette of golfers at sunset
- Badge: "AUSTRALIA'S #1 GROUP TRIP ORGANISER" (small outlined pill)
- H1: "GUYS WEEKENDS" (white bold italic) + "SORTED." (amber bold italic)
- Subtext: "Golf trips, fishing getaways, bucks parties — fully organised end-to-end."
- Two CTAs: "VIEW PACKAGES" (amber fill) + "Start a Trip" (outlined)
- Bottom trust strip: 4 icons with labels (Legendary Itineraries, Cold Beer Waiting, Expert Coordination, 5-Star Reviews)
- Right side: Featured trip card overlay (Murray River Ultimate)

### "Think Group Chat Chaos" Section
- Full-width amber/orange background
- Large bold italic heading in two lines
- Subtext centred

### How It Works (4 Steps)
- Dark background
- Section label: "PROCESS"
- Large ghost numbers (01-04) in background
- 4 columns with icon, step number, bold italic title, description text
- Cards with subtle dark borders

### Packages Section
- Dark background
- Section label: "OUR EXPERIENCES"
- Heading: "CHOOSE YOUR LEGEND" (white + amber)
- 3-column card grid
- Cards: image top, location badge, price overlay, title, checklist, CTA button
- CTA: "LOCK IT IN →" (amber fill)

### Franchise Banner
- Dark background with image
- Bold italic heading: "OWN A BLOKESTRIPS TERRITORY"
- Subtext + single CTA button

### Testimonials
- Section label: "REVIEWS"
- Heading: "REAL BLOKES. REAL TRIPS."
- Quote cards in a row
- Name + trip location below each quote

### Contact/CTA Section
- Dark background
- Heading: "READY TO PLAN YOUR NEXT TRIP?"
- 4 trust bullets on left
- Form on right: Name, Mobile, Email, Trip Type (dropdown), Group Size (dropdown), Notes
- CTA: "SEND ENQUIRY" (amber fill)

### Footer
- Dark background
- Logo + tagline
- 3 columns: Trips, Company, Contact
- Copyright + legal links

## Key Design Principles
- Sharp corners (no rounded cards)
- Bold italic condensed headings
- Amber/orange as primary accent
- Dark charcoal backgrounds
- Ghost/outline numbers as decorative elements
- Strong visual contrast
- Full-width sections with clear separation
- Masculine, premium, sports-travel aesthetic
